BARKSPY ~ A humane barking control alternative

  • BarkSpy is an app that gives automatic feedback in response to your dog's behavior. Watch Video.

  • BarkSpy monitors the sound in your home. When your dog barks, it immediately provides corrective feedback in your voice. When your dog is quiet, BarkSpy provides intermittent positive feedback in your voice.
